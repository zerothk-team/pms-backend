Business workflows
Roles & permissions
KPI definition & library ▼
KPI builderCreate KPIs with name, description, unit (%, $, count, score), measurement frequency (daily/weekly/monthly/quarterly/yearly), and data source (manual, integrated, formula).
Formula engineDefine calculated KPIs using operands from other KPIs or metrics (e.g. Revenue Growth = (Current − Prior) / Prior × 100).
KPI library / templatesPre-built library of common KPIs by department (Sales, HR, Finance, Operations). Users clone and customise.
KPI versioningTrack changes to KPI definitions over time. Preserve historical data under old definition when a KPI is redefined.
Categorisation & taggingOrganise KPIs by department, strategic pillar, owner, or custom tag. Supports multi-level hierarchy.
Target setting ▼
Target assignmentSet targets per KPI at organisation, team, or individual level. Supports fixed values, ranges, and improvement baselines.
Cascading targetsTop-down cascade: break an org-level target into department and individual targets automatically or manually.
WeightingsAssign percentage weights to KPIs so a composite performance score can be calculated per employee or team.
Milestones / checkpointsDefine interim targets at set intervals (e.g. 25% by end of Q1) to track progress during the period.
Stretch targetsOptionally set a stretch target above the base target. Scores above 100% are possible and tracked.
Data collection & progress tracking ▼
Manual entryEmployees or managers input actual values at the configured frequency. Supports bulk upload via CSV.
Integration connectorsAuto-sync from CRMs (Salesforce), HRIS, BI tools, spreadsheets (Google Sheets / Excel), or REST APIs via webhooks.
Progress % calculationAutomatically derive % achieved = (actual / target) × 100 and display vs the current milestone.
Update history & audit logEvery data point timestamped with source (manual/auto) and who submitted it. Full audit trail.
Evidence attachmentsUpload supporting files or links alongside an actual value (e.g. screenshot of CRM report).
Scoring & rating ▼
Scoring scale configAdmin defines rating scale: 1–5 stars, 1–10, letter grades, or custom. Map achievement % ranges to each rating.
Composite scoreWeighted average across all KPIs produces an overall performance score for an employee or team.
NormalisationHandle KPIs where lower is better (e.g. defect rate, cost). Invert scoring direction per KPI.
Period-end lockAt period close, scores are locked, preventing retroactive edits. Admin can unlock with reason.
Dashboards & reporting ▼
Individual KPI dashboardEmployee view: their KPIs, current actuals vs targets, progress bars, trend sparklines, overall score.
Manager team viewSide-by-side comparison of direct reports' KPI progress. Highlight at-risk employees.
Org-level rollupAggregate KPI performance across departments. Drill down from company → department → team → individual.
Historical trend chartsLine/bar charts across multiple periods. Benchmark current performance vs prior periods or peer groups.
Heatmap / distributionBell-curve or grid view of employee scores across the org. Useful for calibration.
Export & scheduled reportsExport to PDF/CSV/Excel. Schedule auto-delivery of reports to stakeholders via email.
Alerts & notifications ▼
At-risk alertsNotify manager + employee when a KPI falls below a configurable threshold (e.g. <60% of target at mid-period).
Update remindersAutomatic nudge when a KPI data entry is overdue. Configurable per frequency (weekly, monthly).
Target achieved / exceededPositive notification when an employee hits 100% or a stretch target.
Period-end remindersRemind managers to review and sign off on KPI actuals before the period closes.
Administration & settings ▼
Review cycle managementConfigure performance cycles (annual, semi-annual, quarterly). Assign KPIs to cycles. Open/close periods.
Role-based access controlHR admin, manager, employee, executive roles with granular permissions on create/view/edit/approve KPIs.
Approval workflowsRequire manager or HR sign-off on KPI definitions, targets, or submitted actuals before they are final.
Org hierarchy syncImport or sync org structure from HRIS. KPI visibility and cascade follows the reporting hierarchy.
1 · KPI creation & approval ▼
1HR admin or manager opens KPI builder, selects from template library or builds from scratch.
2Sets unit, measurement frequency, data source (manual vs integrated), and scoring direction.
3KPI is submitted for approval → HR admin reviews and activates, or returns with comments.
4Active KPI becomes available in the library for assignment to employees or teams.
2 · Target setting & cascade ▼
1Leadership sets org-level targets for the review period.
2System suggests department sub-targets (proportional split or custom). Dept heads adjust and confirm.
3Managers assign KPIs + individual targets to each direct report. Set weightings per KPI.
4Employee reviews their assigned KPIs and targets, acknowledges (optional e-signature).
5Targets are locked — period begins.
3 · In-period progress tracking ▼
1Employee or system submits actual value at each measurement interval (weekly/monthly).
2System calculates % progress vs target and vs milestone. Updates dashboard in real time.
3If below threshold → at-risk alert fires to manager and employee.
4Manager can add coaching note or flag KPI for 1-on-1 discussion.
4 · Period-end scoring & calibration ▼
1Period closes → system calculates final achievement % and weighted composite score per employee.
2Manager reviews computed scores, optionally adds qualitative adjustment with justification.
3HR runs calibration — view distribution heatmap, compare teams, normalise if needed.
4Final scores approved and locked. Feed into compensation, promotion, or development planning modules.
